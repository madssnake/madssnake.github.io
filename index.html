how to play

welcome to the color game!

this game is a brain teaser; one that's designed to challenge your memory.

once you press play, a color will randomly light up. you play your turn by clicking the correct color that was chosen. each round, another square gets added to the sequence. it is your job to then repeat the sequence by clicking the squares in the correct order. you lose when you get the sequence incorrect.

you can also play the game in dark mode, or toggle for different speeds. enjoy!
